# Research Planning Skill

**Skill Name**: research_planning  
**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Description**: Convert problem statement into hypotheses, milestones, and risk controls

---

## Overview

This skill takes a research problem and generates a structured research plan with clear hypotheses, milestones, and risk management strategies.

---

## Capabilities

- 🎯 **Problem Formulation**: Clarify and refine research questions
- 📝 **Hypothesis Generation**: Develop testable hypotheses
- 📅 **Milestone Planning**: Create week-by-week milestones
- ⚠️ **Risk Identification**: Identify potential risks and challenges
- 🛡️ **Mitigation Strategies**: Develop risk mitigation plans
- 📊 **Evaluation Metrics**: Define success criteria and metrics

---

## Usage

### Task Types

This skill handles:
- `task.type = "research_planning"`
- `task.type = "research_plan"`
- `task.type = "proposal_planning"`
- Generic research tasks with keywords matching "plan", "roadmap", "proposal", "hypothesis"

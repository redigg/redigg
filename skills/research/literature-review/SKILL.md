# Literature Review

This skill performs an automated literature review on a given topic.

## Instructions

1.  **Search**: Queries academic databases (e.g. arXiv, Semantic Scholar) for relevant papers.
2.  **Filter**: Selects the most relevant papers based on the topic.
3.  **Analyze**: Extracts key insights, methodologies, and findings.
4.  **Synthesize**: Generates a comprehensive review summary.

## Parameters

- `topic` (required): The research topic or question.

## Examples

- "Do a literature review on Large Language Model reasoning"
- "Survey recent advances in multimodal agents"

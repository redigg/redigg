# Peer Review Skill

**Skill Name**: peer_review  
**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Description**: Structured academic review and decision extraction

---

## Overview

This skill performs structured academic peer review of research proposals and papers.

---

## Capabilities

- 🔍 **Structured Review**: Provide structured academic review
- ✅ **Strengths Identification**: Identify strengths of the work
- ❌ **Weaknesses Identification**: Identify areas for improvement
- 🎯 **Novelty Assessment**: Evaluate novelty and originality
- 📊 **Feasibility Check**: Assess feasibility and practicality
- 📝 **Decision Recommendation**: Provide clear recommendation (Accept/Minor Revision/Major Revision/Reject)

---

## Usage

### Task Types

This skill handles:
- `task.type = "peer_review"`
- Generic research tasks with keywords matching "peer review", "rebuttal", "审稿", "评审"

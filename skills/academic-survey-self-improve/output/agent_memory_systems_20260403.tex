\begin{abstract}
This survey reviews recent advances in Data-Driven Systems Control based on analysis of 30 papers from arXiv (2026-04-02 to 2026-04-02). We identify key trends including rag, retrieval, generation. Our analysis reveals significant progress in rag, with applications spanning multiple domains. We discuss open challenges and outline promising directions for future research.
\end{abstract}

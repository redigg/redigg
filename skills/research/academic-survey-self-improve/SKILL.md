# Academic Survey Self-Improve

## Description
Conducts a comprehensive academic survey on a given topic using arXiv and LLM-based analysis. It iteratively refines the search based on initial findings to improve the quality of the survey.

## Author
vixuowis

## Version
0.1.0

## Usage
- `topic`: The research topic to survey.
- `depth`: (Optional) Depth of the survey (default: "brief").

# Research Highlight Extraction Skill

**Skill Name**: research_highlight_extraction  
**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Description**: Extract key highlights and takeaways from research papers or reports

---

## Overview

This skill extracts key highlights, findings, and takeaways from research papers and reports.

---

## Capabilities

- 🔑 **Key Findings Extraction**: Extract key findings from papers
- 📝 **Summary Generation**: Generate concise summaries
- 💡 **Takeaway Identification**: Identify actionable takeaways
- 📊 **Result Extraction**: Extract key results and conclusions
- 🎯 **Contribution Identification**: Identify key contributions
- 📋 **Bullet Point Generation**: Generate structured bullet points

---

## Usage

### Task Types

This skill handles:
- `task.type = "research_highlight_extraction"`
- `task.type = "highlight_extraction"`
- `task.type = "takeaway_extraction"`
- Generic research tasks with keywords matching "highlight", "takeaway", "key finding", "summary", "亮点", "要点", "摘要", "总结"
